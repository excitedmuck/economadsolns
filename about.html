Our challenge

Agricultural technology has made great strides in converting farm waste into renewable fuel and natural fertiliser, but in Europe, this is only widely available to large scale farming operations. We believe that efficient waste management solutions should be widely available and we set ourselves on the mission to help small farms to improve the sustainability of their resource management and agricultural waste recycling while enabling their autonomy and self-sufficiency.

Our passion

Our passion is to develop sustainable, affordable and reliable solutions that manage resources for smallholding farms and rural communities. We are creating cutting-edge systems for converting farm waste into renewable fuel, among other sustainable technologies and making it accessible to everyone.
